About Us

Honoring a legacy of knowledge, compassion, and service

The Akhiezer Heritage Foundation is a US non-profit organization registered in the State of Connecticut. We are driven by the belief that education, compassion, and scientific inquiry can create lasting, meaningful change in people's lives.

Our Mission

01

Honoring a Remarkable Legacy

We carry forward the spirit of Naum Akhiezer — an outstanding Ukrainian mathematician, scientist, educator, and humanist. His life's work and deeply held values continue to inspire everything we do.

02

Humanitarian Relief

We provide support to vulnerable communities affected by war, displacement, and natural disasters. Through direct aid and grants to qualified nonprofit organizations, we help people in their most difficult moments.

03

Education & Recovery

We support educational, scientific, and training programs that help communities rebuild and thrive. By investing in long-term recovery and economic stability, we help create foundations for a better future.

04

Advancing STEM

We champion education and research in science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and related fields — opening doors for the next generation of thinkers, innovators, and problem-solvers.

Naum Akhiezer (1901–1980)

Who Was Naum Akhiezer?

Naum Ilyich Akhiezer (1901–1980) was one of the most distinguished Ukrainian mathematicians of the twentieth century. His pioneering work in approximation theory, the theory of operators, and mathematical physics earned him recognition across the global scientific community.

But Akhiezer was more than a brilliant scholar. He was a generous mentor, a beloved teacher, and a person of deep humanity. He spent decades nurturing young minds at Kharkiv University, shaping generations of mathematicians and scientists. His conviction that knowledge should serve people — not just ideas — is the principle at the heart of this Foundation.
